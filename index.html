What is EngageZA

Your organisation, always connected

EngageZA gives any organisation a branded communication hub — like having a dedicated app for your community, without the cost or complexity.

  • No coding, no IT team needed. Set up your branded page in minutes using our simple dashboard.

  • Your brand, your colours. Upload your logo and we handle the rest — it looks like yours.

  • Works on any device. Community members access it from their phone, tablet, or desktop — no app download required.

  • Built-in AI assistant. An intelligent chatbot answers your members' questions 24/7, even when you're offline.
