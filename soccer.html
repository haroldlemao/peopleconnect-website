Our Club

Football for Every Generation

Absalom Tete FC — Batshweneng was founded in 2023 with a clear mission: to give every young footballer in Boksburg a home. From our youngest Under 6 players to our Veterans (35+), we are committed to developing skill, discipline, and a lifelong love for the beautiful game.

We believe football builds more than players — it builds character, community, and confidence. Whether your child is picking up a ball for the first time, or you're a seasoned veteran looking to stay in the game, there is a place for you at Absalom Tete FC.

Code of Conduct

Our Standards

Respect, discipline, and sportsmanship are the foundation of everything we do at Absalom Tete FC.

⚽
Player Code of Conduct
●Be on time for training and matches
●Wear full kit (including shin guards)
●Show respect and sportsmanship at all times
●Follow coach instructions
👨‍👩‍👧
Parents' Responsibilities
●Encourage positive behaviour
●Do not coach from the sidelines
●Respect referees and officials
●Support all players
🗓️
Game Day Rules
●Respect referee decisions
●Show sportsmanship (e.g. handshakes after matches)
●Spectators stay in designated areas
⚠️ Late arrival fine: R50 — payable on arrival
